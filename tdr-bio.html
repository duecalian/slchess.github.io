T.D.R. Peiris Bio

Personal Info.

Name : T. D. R. Peiris.

Full Name : Telge Dantha Ravindra Peiris.

Date of Birth : 10th October 1958.

Address : 346/8, Mahawatta Road, Arrawwala, Pannipitiya.

Telephone : 0719, 311 576

Education : G.C.E- O/L, 06 Credits, 01 Ordinary Pass.

Mr. TDR Peiris, (Former National Chess Champion, Coach and Chess Writer who has produced so many National Players and gained wide recognition Internationally)


Chess Achievements.

1981- Received international Rating, 2225, in zonal game of the 9th World Chess Championship in Dubi

1980- 2006, Member of the Sri Lanka National Chess Teem.

1980- Sri Lanka National Chess Champion.

1981- Sri Lanka National Runner up.

1982- Sri Lanka National 2nd Runner up.

1983- Sri Lanka National Chess Champion.

1983- Sri Lanka Open Chess Champion.

1995- Sri Lanka National Chess Champion.

2004- Sri Lanka National Chess Champion.

2005- Sri Lanka National Chess Champion.

2010- Sri Lanka National (B)Chess Champion.

1982,1984,1986,1988 & 2000- Represented Sri Lanka in the Chess Olympiad.

2002,2004,& 2006- selected to play in the Chess Olympiad but could not participate,

1980 to 2002, Represented Sri Lanka in Chess Asian Games.


Chess Records.

1980- 1st Player to win only one of Asian International Chess Master Amanuel Aren from India.

1980- 1st Player to draw with National Chess Couch with Yugoslavian I. M. Mr. Joksic

1981- 1st Player to achieve an International rating.

1983- 1st Player to win National & Open, Closed Championship in the same year.

2004- 1st Player to win National A and B Titles in the same year.

2006 to 2010, - 1st Player to play against two sons and student consecutive year in Sri Lanka National Chess Championships.

2014, 15, 16, 17,-1st Player to win Senior National Continuously Championship.

2016- 1st Chess Player to Wrought Chess School Syllabus in Sri Lanka.

1997- Chess Coaching University of Sri Jayewardenepura -Inter University Champions.

2006- Chess Coach, University of Kelaniya.4th place, Inter University Championship.


Chess Coaching Schools.

1980 to 1983, 2000 to 2006- Chess Coach,D.S.Senanayaka College, Colombo 8 in 1985, my student Ishan Weerakoon became Sri Lanka National Champion.3 players received international ratings. Thrice won the Inter School Championship.

2000 to 2006- Chess Coach, Ananda College,Colombo 10-Inter School Championships.

1995 to 1998, Chess Coach,President College, Kotte(Didula de Silva became Sri Lanka National Champion,Inter School Championships).

2000 to 2011- Dharmapala College, pannipitiya(Raveen Thrimavitarana become Sri Lanka Junior National Champion,T.Gayan Peiris became runner-up in Sri Lanka National Championship(Premier Division),T.Sajith Peiris. 2010 and consequently he got selected to the National Team won Candidate Master Title in the Chess Olympiad in Russia,03 time Inter School Chess Champions).

2011 to date- Chess coach Thurston College-Western province school champions.
